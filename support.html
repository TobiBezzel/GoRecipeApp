Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new recipe?
Tap the '+' button in the Recipes tab, fill in the recipe details including ingredients, instructions, and cooking time. Don't forget to add a photo to make your recipe more appealing!
Can I access my recipes offline?
Yes! All your created and saved recipes are stored locally on your device, so you can access them anytime, even without an internet connection.
How do I backup my recipes?
Go to Settings > Backup & Restore. You can export all your recipes to a file that can be imported later or on another device.
How do I share a recipe with friends?
Open any recipe and tap the share icon. You can share via messaging apps, email, or social media platforms installed on your device.
Can I edit a recipe after creating it?
Absolutely! Open the recipe and tap the edit icon in the top right corner. Make your changes and save.
Is my data secure and private?
Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or share your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.

Tips & Tricks

Take Great Recipe Photos Use natural lighting and take photos from different angles. A good photo makes your recipe more attractive and easier to identify.
Organize with Tags Use tags like "quick", "vegetarian", or "dessert" to categorize your recipes and find them faster.
Set Cooking Timers Use the built-in timer feature when following a recipe to keep track of cooking and prep times.
Save Your Favorites Mark recipes as favorites for quick access. Perfect for your go-to meals and most-loved dishes.
